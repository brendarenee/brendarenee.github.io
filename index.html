About

Brenda Butler

I am a front-end web developer with a passion for working with small businesses and non-profits to establish modern, easy-to-navigate websites. I strive to enhance visibility, accessibility, and facilitate relationships within the community. I would love to collaborate with you!

Background

In 2007, I graduated from Humboldt State University with a degree in Sociology and a minor in Spanish. For the next ten years I worked in social services, first working with foster youth and then working with the SNAP (formerly Food Stamps) program determining eligibility. I loved the work I did, but I knew I wanted to take the leap to web development, even if I had to work my way all the way up. In 2018, I began focusing full-time on my education at Team Treehouse, while also taking community college classes to supplement.

And then my life took some detours -- I became a full-time mom and also took part-time work as a Library Assistant. But in pursuit of making the most of the bananas year that was 2020, I completed my Front-End Web Development program through Team Treehouse, while relocating to Bend, OR last fall with my family. My web development education continues, but I am excited to start working with you!
