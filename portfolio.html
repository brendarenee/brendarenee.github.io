Some would call this a mid-career change and they would be right.

In 2018, I began focusing full-time on my education at Team Treehouse, while also taking classes at the local community college to supplement my education.

In pursuit of making the most of this bananas year that has been 2020, I've completed my Front-End Web Development program through Team Treehouse, while relocating to Bend, OR with my family. Here I hope to continue my education, while helping local businesses create an engaging online presence.
